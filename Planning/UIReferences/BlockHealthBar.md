## Block Health Bar

![Block Health Bar](../images/ui/block-health-bar.png)

- Appears at the top center of the screen when mining a block
- Shows the ore type name (e.g., "Diamond") in stylized white font with black outline
- Displays a horizontal health bar with:
  - Dark blue rectangular outline
  - Green fill on the left representing current health
  - Dark blue fill on the right representing missing health
  - Health text overlay showing "current/max HP" (e.g., "7,370/11,881 HP") in white font with black outline
- The background has geometric, angular shapes resembling ice or crystals, consistent with a mining theme
- Use this when displaying block health during mining to show progress
